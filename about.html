Hi, I'm Mark

I created this website to showcase some of my engineering projects, and document my more interesting outdoor excursions.

I currently work at Tesla where I am excited to work with some of the smartest people in the world on electrifying our transportation infrastructure. In my spare time, I enjoy running, backpacking, cocktail making, guitar playing, and eating my way through San Francisco.
